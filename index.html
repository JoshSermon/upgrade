About Faith Conference

The beautiful city of Atlanta, Georgia will be the host city for Faith Conference!

Discover your purpose with a lineup of well-known pastors. They will teach you new techniques to advance your career and align your vision with God.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to network with believers and build a strong Godly community for the kingdom? Be the first to know what that experience will be like.

Faith Conference is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and worshipping.

What You'll Learn

Faith: Faith in God Vision: Vision for your future Purpose: Purpose with a plan Obedience: Following God completely Prayer: Prayer through ups and downs Finance: Good steward with money

Speakers

William Murphy

William is a servant of God, husband, father, international gospel recording artist, and pastor of The dReam Center Church of Atlanta.

Christopher Chappell

Dr. Christopher is the founder and pastor of the Grace Community Christian Church in Kennesaw, Georgia, with a second location in Austell, Georgia.

Creflo Dollar

Dr. Creflo is the founder and senior pastor of World Changers Church International (WCCI) in College Park, Georgia, which serves nearly 30,000 members.

Jamal Bryant

Jamal is an African-American pastor who as of December 2018 is the senior pastor of the New Birth Missionary Baptist Church where he has become very successful.

Paul Morton

Bishop Morton now serves as Senior Pastor of the “baby” church, G.S.S. Changing A Generation, in Atlanta, Georgia which was birthed out of the mother church.

Dale Bronner

Bishop Bronner is the founder/senior pastor of Word of Faith Family Worship Cathedral, an interdenominational ministry, founded in 1991.
